How It Works

The process of selling your car with CarDeal is easy and straightforward. Our employees will keep you informed so you will understand what happens next.

Tell us about your vehicle

Fill out a form that provides accurate information about your car (i.e., VIN, year, make, model, mileage, condition of car, accidents).

Get your offer

Get an instant offer on your car that is good for 7 days.

Provide access to car

Deliver car to closest CarDeal location or have us come get it.

Documentation

Provide title proving your ownership of the vehicle.

Inspection

CarDeal will inspect your car to verify its condition.

Get Paid!

If everything checks out, you can decide if you want to accept the offer. You can go with cash or apply the money to a trade-in.

The Advantages of Selling Your Car to CarDeal

If you're like most people, you don't look forward to dealing with a pushy car salesperson. CarDeal offers a better way for you to sell or trade in your car. See the following reasons people choose CarDeal.

  • Our offers are very competitive; you'll get more money for your car than a standard dealer trade-in.
  • We are happy to take off your hands "less-than-perfect" cars that have body damage, mechanical problems, or excess wear and tear.
  • We'll buy your car and you aren't required to buy one from us.
  • Avoid getting ripped off by scammers and swindlers. We make the process of selling your car fast, easy, and safe.
  • CarDeal employees are dedicated and professional. This has earned us an A+ BBB rating plus thousands of reviews from satisfied customers.
  • Make and model driven on American roads.
  • We have locations in all 50 states making it easy for you to deliver your car or have us come to you to pick up your car.
  • CarDeal cares about people as much as cars. We have donated over a million meals to Feeding America and Meals on Wheels.
